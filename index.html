Executive MBA · Health Management Programme
The Institute of Health Management & Research Sciences (IHMRS) at Liaquat University of Medical & Health Sciences (LUMHS), Jamshoro, is dedicated to producing highly skilled health management professionals. Established under one of Pakistan's oldest and most prestigious medical universities — founded in 1881 — IHMRS offers the Executive MBA in Health Management, equipping healthcare leaders with advanced administrative and research competencies.

Platform Features
Everything You Need
A complete assessment management system — no cost, no installation, works on any device.
📹
Webinar-Linked Assessments
Each quiz is linked to a specific webinar video. Students watch before they answer — ensuring genuine engagement.
⏱️
Video Watch Verification
The platform verifies that students have watched the video before the quiz is unlocked.
🔀
Randomised Questions
Each participant receives a unique set of 10 questions from the full question bank — no two quizzes are alike.
📋
Detailed Answer Review
After submission, students see a full review of all questions with correct answers and explanations.
🏅
Personalised Certificates
Certificates are personalised with the participant's name and carry official LUMHS-IHMRS institutional branding.
✅
Online Verification
Every certificate carries a unique ID that can be verified instantly by employers or institutions.
📊
Performance Dashboard
Faculty can track all student scores, pass rates, watch times, and certificate issuance from one dashboard.
📤
Manual Question Upload
Teachers can add questions manually or upload a CSV file — full control over the question bank at all times.
